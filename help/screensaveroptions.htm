Screensaver Options


The Screensaver Options section of the General Settings area allows you to decide how screensavers should be played back.  There are two possibilities:

 

• Show one screensaver per playback session – This setting means that, each time your screensaver starts, ProSaver will choose one screensaver which will run continuously until you close it, either by pressing a key or moving the mouse.

 

• Switch screensavers during playback – This setting will show your screensavers as a “slideshow”.  In other words, when your screensaver starts, ProSaver will choose one screensaver and run it.  The screensaver will be displayed for the number of minutes selected in the ‘Show each screensaver for’ box.  Then, ProSaver will select another screensaver and display it.  This process will continue until you close the screensaver by pressing a key or moving the mouse.
