Part A: The ProSaver Main Window


The ProSaver Main Window is the where all the action takes place!  From here, you can:

 

• View a list of all the screensavers that are available on your computer.

 

• Arrange your favorite screensavers into playlists that will be displayed when your computer gets bored.

 

• Organize your screensavers into categories and give them ratings and useful descriptions.

 

• Add background music to turn a good screensaver a great screensaver!

 

• Set options for the ProSaver Playback Window to use when displaying your screensavers.

 

• Set keyboard shortcuts (hotkeys) that allow you to quickly access ProSaver’s most important features.

 

 

To use the ProSaver Main Window, the first thing you need to do is open it!  Perform the following steps to open the ProSaver Main Window:

 

1. Arrange the windows on your screen so that you can see your wallpaper (your Desktop).

 

2. Right-click on the Desktop to open a popup menu.

 

3. Near the bottom of the menu is the word ‘Properties’.  Click on it.  The Display Properties window should now be open.

 

4. Look along the top of the Display Properties window and click the 'Screen Saver' tab.

 

5. This step is very important: In order for ProSaver Screensaver Manager to work properly, it must be set as your current screensaver!  If you don’t see 'ProSaver Screensaver Manager' displayed here, do this:

 

      a. Click the arrow to bring down the screensaver list.

 

      b. Look for ‘ProSaver Screensaver Manager’ in the list and click on it.

 

      c. Click the Apply button.

 

6. With ProSaver as your default screensaver, click the Settings button to open the Main Window.

 

7. And there you have it!  When you’re done looking around, click the OK button to close the window.

 

8. Now look at the System Tray, in the lower-right section of your screen.  You should see the ProSaver icon (a yellow circle with the letters ‘P’ and ‘S’ inside) displayed there.

 

9. Right-click the icon to open a popup menu.

 

10. Click on ‘Open ProSaver Options’ in the menu.

 

11. And that’s the easy way to open the Main Window!

 

12. Close the ProSaver window again.

 

13. And now for the really easy way:  As long as you haven’t changed any of settings yet, you can just press Ctrl+Alt+P to open the Main Window instantly.  Go ahead, try it!  Hold the Ctrl+Alt keys, and press the letter ‘P’.  Viola!

 

 

Now that you are an expert at opening the ProSaver Main Window, it’s time to move on to the next section.
