Part B: Using Keyboard Shortcuts


ProSaver Screensaver Manager offers a handful of useful keyboard shortcuts, both for regular operations and for screensaver playback, that can help make your life just a little easier (and, with the proper screensavers, maybe even a little more fun!)  Here’s how to experience them for yourself:

 

Note:  This section of the Tutorial assumes that you haven’t changed any of the hotkey settings.  If you have made any changes, you will have to use your own key combinations when they are called for in the Tutorial.

 

1. First of all, make sure the keyboard shortcuts (hotkeys) are enabled, as described in Tutorial #3: Setting Playback Options.

 

2. Now, try the first one: Ctrl+Alt+S to instantly display the next screensaver in your playlist.  Similar to the System Tray Icon that was discussed in Part A of this Tutorial, this quick hotkey combination is useful whenever you want privacy on your Windows Desktop, or when you just need to kick back and watch some screensavers for a few minutes.

 

3. The next one to try is: Ctrl+Alt+P to open the ProSaver Main Window.  This is an even faster and easier way to get to your playlist settings whenever you need them.

 

4. This one will only work while the screensaver is running, so you will want to use the ‘Start screensaver’ hotkey first.  Then use: Ctrl+Alt+N to display the next screensaver in your playlist, a nice feature for showing off all of your favorites.

 

5. This brings us to the audio Playback Controls.  Again, make sure the controls are enabled as described in Tutorial #5: Adding Music and Audio Playlists.

 

6. With the screensaver running (don’t forget the ‘Start screensaver’ hotkey), use Ctrl+Right to skip to the next song in your playlist (another showing-off feature), Ctrl+Up and Ctrl+Down to adjust the volume, and Ctrl+M if you need to mute the volume.

 

 

Congratulations!  You have just completed Tutorial #6: Shortcuts and Time-Savers.  With this arsenal of time-saving tools at your disposal, you’ll be running screensavers and setting options like never before!  And now, if you dare, you are invited to move on to the last ProSaver Tutorial:  Tutorial #7: Using Advanced Options.
