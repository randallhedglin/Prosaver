DirectX Audio Effects


There are two settings for DirectX Audio Effects in the Synthesizer Options Box:

 

• Reverb – Select this option to add reverberation, an echo-like quality, to MIDI and WAV files.  This gives audio the effect of being played in a large concert hall, and is a nice enhancement for most songs.

 

• Chorus – Select this option to add a chorus effect to MIDI and WAV songs.  This effect gives the audio a richer, fuller sound and is very pleasing to the ears.

 

It is recommended that both of these options be enabled for maximum enjoyment of MIDI and WAV file playback.
