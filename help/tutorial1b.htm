Part B: The Screensavers Tab


The Screensavers Tab is where you gain access to All Things Screensaver.  You can do the following things here:

 

• See a list of all of your screensavers.

 

• Create, edit, save, and load screensaver playlists for your own viewing pleasure.

 

• Add information (such as theme, rating, and description) to your screensavers.

 

• Change settings and preview your screensavers (including background music!).

 

 

The Screensavers Tab is described in much more detail in Tutorial #2: Creating a Screensaver Playlist.

 

For now, here’s something fun to try:

 

1. Open the ProSaver Main Window.  (Come on, you didn’t forget already, did you?)

 

2. Find your favorite screensaver in the Screensaver List and click on it.

 

3. Some information will be displayed about the screensaver, and you will see the Mini-Preview Window on the lower-right spring to life.

 

4. Click on the Settings button.  This will open the screensaver’s default properties window.  Close this window when done.

 

5. Now, click the Preview button.  You will see a full-screen preview of the screensaver.

 

 

It’s that simple!  Now, let’s move on to the next section.
