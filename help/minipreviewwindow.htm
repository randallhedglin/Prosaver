Mini-Preview Window


The Mini-Preview Window displays a small preview of the selected screensaver.  If you prefer, you can disable this option by clearing the ‘Preview’ checkbox.

 

(Note:  In some cases where the mini-preview for a certain screensaver causes problems with the program, you may want to disable the mini-preview for that screensaver only.  To do this, click the Edit Info button to open the Screensaver Info Editor.)
