Part E: Assigning Playlists to Screensavers


By now, you should have a music playlist ready to go, with the name ‘Space Songs.apl’.   The last step in this Tutorial will help you to assign this list to a screensaver, specifically the Starfield (or Flying Through Space) screensaver that we started out with.  Here are the steps to take:

 

1. Open the ProSaver Main Window one more time.

 

2. Find the screensaver named ‘Starfield’ or ‘Flying Through Space’ (the one we used earlier in this Tutorial) in the Screensaver List and click on it.

 

3. In the Current Selection Box, click the Edit Info button to open the Screensaver Info Editor (shown above).

 

4. Click the Change button to open the Change Music Dialog.

 

5. Now, we are going to load the audio playlist that we created earlier in this Tutorial, so click the Load ProSaver Audio Playlist button.

 

6. Using the window that pops up, find the ‘Space Songs.apl’ playlist and Open it.

 

7. Click the OK button to save your changes and close the Screensaver Info Editor.

 

8. Now, all is ready!  Back in the Main Window, in the Current Selection Box, click the Preview button to start the screensaver and listen to the music!

 

9. Click the Preview button again, but this time we’re going to try out the Playback Controls.

 

10. After the music starts playing, try each of the playback controls (assuming you haven’t changed any of them):

 

            a. Ctrl+Right Arrow: Skip to the next song in the playlist.

 

            b. Ctrl+Up Arrow:  Turn up the audio volume.

 

            c. Ctrl+Down Arrow:  Turn down the audio volume.

 

            d. Ctrl+M:  Mute the audio volume.

 

 

Congratulations!  You have just completed Tutorial #5: Adding Music and Audio Playlists.  You can now use what you have just learned to create playlists of your choice for any of the screensavers in your collection.  You can even use ProSaver to replace the music that comes with some screensavers.  With this, you are ready to continue with Tutorial #6: Shortcuts and Time-Savers.
