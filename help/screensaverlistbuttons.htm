Screensaver List Buttons


There are two buttons which accompany the Screensaver List:

 

• Clear Selection – Click this button to clear the currently highlighted screensaver.  (This will clear the Current Selection Box and stop the Mini-Preview Window.)

 

• Add to Playlist – Click this button to add the highlighted screensaver to the end of the Screensaver Playlist.
