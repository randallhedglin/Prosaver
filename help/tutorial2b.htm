Part B: Arranging the Playlist


The Current Playlist Box, in the upper-right corner of the Screensavers Tab, is where you manage the Screensaver Playlist.  (The Screensaver Playlist always displays the screensavers that will be shown by the ProSaver Playback Window when your screensaver is activated.  This list can be modified at any time, saved to a file, and another list can be created or loaded from file to replace it.  If the Current Playlist Box is empty, the ProSaver Default Screensaver will be shown.)

 

Here are some things to try with the Screensaver Playlist:

 

1. Right-click the list to open a popup menu.  Move the mouse pointer to ‘Sort Items by’, and then, in the submenu, click on ‘Name (A-Z)’.  The screensavers will now be arranged in alphabetical order.

 

2. You can do the same thing using the ‘Sort Items by’ box directly underneath the Screensaver Playlist.  Click on the arrow to drop the list, then click on ‘Name (Z-A)’.  The list will now be sorted in reverse alphabetical order.

 

3. Next, click on a screensaver in the playlist.  Using the Move Selection Buttons, practice moving the screensaver up, down, to the top, and to the bottom.

 

4. You can also use some keyboard shortcuts to arrange items in the playlist:  Highlight an item using the up and down arrows keys.  If you hold down the Shift key while moving up and down, the selected screensaver will move with you.  You can also hold the Shift+Ctrl keys while using the arrow keys to move the screensaver to the top or bottom of the playlist.

 

5. Use the Delete button to remove one or two screensavers from the list.

 

6. Now add all of your favorite screensavers to the playlist again, and spend some time arranging them in just the right order.

 

 

With a nice playlist in hand, arranged just the way you like it, your are now ready to move on to the next section.
