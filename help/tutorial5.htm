Tutorial #5: Adding Music and Audio Playlists


Perhaps one of the most useful features of ProSaver Screensaver Manager (at least from an artistic point-of-view) is the ability to add background music to the vast majority of screensavers.  This Tutorial will guide you step-by-step through the process of using ProSaver to do exactly this.

 

Tutorial #5 is divided into the following sections:

 

• Part A: The Change Music Dialog

 

• Part B: Using the Audio File List

 

• Part C: Arranging the Playlist

 

• Part D: Playback Controls and Options

 

• Part E: Assigning Playlists to Screensavers
