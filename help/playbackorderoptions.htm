Playback Order Options


The Playback Order section of the General Settings area allows you to decide in what order ProSaver will display screensavers from your playlist.  There are two options:

 

• Display playlist screensavers in order – This option will cause screensavers to be shown in the exact order that they appear in the Screensaver Playlist.  After the last screensaver in the list is played, the list will be replayed from the beginning.

 

• Select random screensavers from playlist – This option will select random screensavers from your playlist as they are displayed.  (This is similar to the ‘shuffle’ setting on most CD players.)  ProSaver is designed so that (unless there are invalid screensavers in the list) the same screensaver will never be shown two times in a row.
