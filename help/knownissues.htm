Known Issues


• Depending on the speed and/or configuration of you computer, it may take an extended period of time (generally no more than 30 seconds) to open folders containing numerous files and/or subfolders while using the Music Manager.  You may also see the Music Manager window redraw itself a few times while such folders are loading.  This is a natural result of the program's operation, and our programmers have taken extra steps to ensure that the application does not report itself as 'Not Responding' during this time period.  If you see 'Not Responding' in the ProSaver title bar, then there is a problem.  Otherwise, wait patiently and rest assured that the folder's contents will appear as soon as they have been loaded.

 

• On most systems, the audio hotkeys will not be available for the first few seconds of each screensaver's playback.  This is not considered a bug in the application, but is simply the result of several operations that must take place in the background during the playback process.  You can consider the audio hotkeys ready to be used only after music playback has started.  (Attempting to use the hotkeys before this will, in most cases, close the screensaver.)

 

• There are two situations in which the audio hotkeys will not work.  Both of these are known to occur only under Windows XP:  (1) When the 'Password protect' box is checked under the screensaver configuration in Windows Control Panel, and (2) When the screensaver starts while the login screen is displayed.  Both of these issues are related to Windows security, and are not considered bugs.  (As part of its security features, Windows XP blocks keyboard input to all third-party applications during the password and login screens.  You will notice that any hotkeys for your other applications will not function during these times either.)  A workaround for situation (1) is to turn off password protection for screensavers under Windows Control Panel.  There is no workaround for situation (2); the hotkeys will function normally only after a successful login.
