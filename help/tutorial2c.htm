Part C: Previewing the Playlist


This brief section of the Tutorial will assist you in previewing the playlist you just created in Part B.  Here’s what to do:

 

1. Click the OK button to close ProSaver Screensaver Manager.  (Don’t click the Cancel button!  This will cause you to lose any changes that you have made to the Screensaver Playlist.)

 

2. Open the Windows Display Properties dialog box.  (A quick and easy way to do this is to right-click the ProSaver System Tray Icon, usually in the lower-right corner of your screen, and click on ‘Show Display Properties’ in the popup menu.

 

3. In the Display Properties box, make sure you are looking at the ‘Screen Saver’ tab.

 

4. Once again, make sure ProSaver Screensaver Manager is set as your current screensaver.

 

5. Click the Preview button.  The first screensaver in your list will be displayed!

 

6. Move the mouse or press a key to close the screensaver.

 

7. Click the Preview button again.  Now you see the second screensaver.  (How exciting is this?!  Please, try to contain yourself.)

 

8. Continue performing these steps as many times as you like.

 

 

Now, open the ProSaver Main Window again and get ready to move on to the next section.
