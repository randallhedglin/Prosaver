Part B: Hotkey Controls


This brief section will show you how to use ProSaver’s hotkey controls.  These controls save you time by letting you access certain features of ProSaver very easily.  Here are some ideas:

 

• You’ve just downloaded a new screensaver, and you would like to look at its settings and add it to the current playlist.  Just hit the ‘Open ProSaver options’ hotkey and you are ready to go!

 

• You are leaving your desk and would like to keep what you are working on private.  Press the ‘Start screensaver’ hotkey and be on your way!

 

• You have a great screensaver collection and would like to show it off to your friends or colleagues.  Hit the ‘Start screensaver’ hotkey to get things rolling, then use the ‘Skip to next screensaver’ hotkey to move through each of your awesome screensavers!

 

 

As time goes by, you will probably think of even more clever uses.  For now, here’s how to use the hotkeys:

 

1. Open the ProSaver Main Window and click on the Playback Options Tab.

 

2. In the General Settings section, find the Hotkeys box.

 

3. Make sure the ‘Enable hotkeys for the following operations’ box is checked, as shown above.

 

4. Take a look at the three boxes below this option.  These are your screensaver hotkey settings.  If you haven’t changed them, they will be as follows:

 

            a. Start screensaver: Ctrl+Alt+S

 

            b. Skip to next screensaver: Ctrl+Alt+N

 

            c. Open ProSaver options: Ctrl+Alt+P

 

5. For now, leave the settings as they are.  Click the OK button to save your changes and close the window.

 

6. Now, let’s bring ProSaver back:  Hold the Control and Alt keys, and press the ‘P’ key.  (If you have changed this setting, then you will have to use whatever combination that you have set.)

 

7. Zoinks!  Would you look at that?  The ProSaver Main Window is back.

 

8. Now, try the ‘Start screensaver’ hotkey.  Hold Ctrl+Alt and press ‘S’.

 

9. How convenient!  Now for the last one:  While the screensasver is running (use Ctrl+Alt+S again if you need to), use the ‘Skip to next screensaver’ hotkey (Ctrl+Alt+N) to change screensavers.  (Please note that you must wait a few seconds before the hotkey will work.  Otherwise, you might confuse the screensaver, and it will close!)

 

10. And that’s all there is to it.  If you want to change any of the hotkey settings (such as if any of your other programs are trying to use the same hotkeys), just click in the box and use your own combination of the Control, Alt or Shift keys (along with one other key) to set a new one.

 

 

Congratulations!  You have just completed Tutorial #3: Setting Playback Options.  ProSaver Screensaver Manager will now obey your every wish.  (Well, almost every wish.  We’re still working on the ‘Clean my house’ setting.)  You are now ready to continue with Tutorial #4: Organizing Your Screensavers.
