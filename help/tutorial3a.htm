Part A: Playback Settings


ProSaver Screensaver Manager allows you to choose from a number of settings so that your screensavers will be played back in just the way you prefer.  You can set the following options:

 

• Choose to display only one screensaver each time ProSaver runs, or display them as a slideshow, one after another, each one for as long as you want.

 

• Play back screensavers from your playlist in order, or let ProSaver select random screensavers from your list.

 

• Select and use hotkey controls that allow you access important ProSaver features with a simple key combination.

 

• Decide whether or not to clear the screen before running screensavers, and temporarily turn off background music for your screensavers.

 

 

Here’s how to do it:

 

1. Open the ProSaver Main Window.

 

2. Make sure you have a playlist with at least two or three screensavers displayed in the Current Playlist Box.  (If not, see Tutorial #2: Creating a Screensaver Playlist.)

 

3. Click on the Playback Options Tab near the top of the window.

 

4. Look in the General Settings section and find the Screensavers box.

 

5. Click on the ‘Switch screensavers during playback’ option.

 

6. In the ‘Show each screensaver for’ box, enter ‘1’ for one minute.

 

7. Click the OK button to save your changes and close the ProSaver Main Window.

 

8. Now, open the Windows Display Properties dialog.  (Tip: Right-click on the ProSaver System Tray Icon and select ‘Show display properties’ from the popup menu.)

 

9. Under the ‘Screen Saver’ tab, make sure ProSaver is set as your current screensaver and click the Preview button.

 

10.  A screensaver from your playlist will be displayed.  After approximately one minute (which is what we set earlier, remember?), the next screensaver in the list will be displayed.  Magic, eh?

 

 

You can return to the ProSaver Main Window and change the settings in the Screensavers box to whatever you would like.  If you want the screensavers to change like this, leave the setting as it is.  You can change the ‘Show each screensaver for’ value to anything you wish, up to 120 minutes.  (Although 3 or 4 minutes is usually a good number.)  If you would rather only see one screensaver, then change the setting back to ‘Show one screensaver per playback session’.

 

Next, let’s try this:

 

1. Open the ProSaver Main Window again.

 

2. Click on the Playback Options Tab.

 

3. In the General Settings section, look for the Playback Order box.

 

4. Click on the option to ‘Display playlist screensavers in order’.

 

5. Now, click OK to close the ProSaver Main Window.

 

6. Open the Windows Display Properties dialog once again.

 

7. Click the Preview button to display a screensaver from your playlist.

 

8. Move the mouse or press a key to close the screensaver.

 

9. Click Preview again and you will see the very next screensaver in your list.

 

10. Repeat as many times as you would like.

 

11. Click the Settings button to open the ProSaver Main Window.

 

12. Go back to the Playback Options Tab and click the ‘Select random screensavers from the playlist’ option.

 

13.  Click OK to save your changes and close the window.

 

14. Go back to Windows Display Properties and click the Preview button a few times.  With this setting, you never know what you’re going to get!

 

 

Now that you are an expert, you can go back and change the settings to anything you desire.  When you are done, we will move on to the next section.
