Other Options


There are two additional options as well as two buttons that can be found in the General Settings section of the Playback Options Tab.

 

The two additional options are:

 

• Clear screen before running screensavers – If this option is selected, the desktop will be cleared before screensaver playback begins.  Otherwise, the desktop will remain visible.

 

• Turn off ProSaver music playback in all screensavers – Select this option if, for any reason, you do not want music playback to be included when displaying screensavers.  (Setting this option will leave all of your audio settings and playlists intact so that you can later turn music playback on without any hassle.)

 

The two additional buttons are:

 

• Open Themes Manager – Click this button to display the Themes Manager window.

 

• Open Music Manager – Click this button to display the Music Manager window.
