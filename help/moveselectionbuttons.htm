Move Selection Buttons


The Move Selection Buttons allow you to arrange screensavers in the Screensaver Playlist.  There are five buttons:

 

• Move Up – Click to move the selected screensaver up one position in the list.

 

• Move Down – Click to move the selected screensaver down one position in the list.

 

• Move to Top – Click to move the selected screensaver to the top of the list.

 

• Move to Bottom – Click to move the selected screensaver to the bottom of the list.

 

• Delete – Click to remove the selected screensaver from the playlist.
