About This Software

 

  What's your favorite screensaver?  Do you have a hard time picking just one?  If so, you're not alone!  With the literally thousands of screensavers that can be downloaded right now, most of us have several that we enjoy watching.  Whether it's gently flowing waterfalls, morphing geometrics, or blasting enemy spaceships in the outer reaches of space, it can be hard to pick just one.  And any user of Windows knows that it can be a real hassle to change your screensaver on a frequent basis.

 

  And that's exactly why you need ProSaver Screensaver Manager from WaveBreak Software!  ProSaver is a full-featured screensaver management and enhancement application.  ProSaver will make your life easier and more fun in many ways:

 

• Run all of your favorite screensavers without ever having to bother with the tedious Windows Control Panel.

 

• Quickly and easily create playlists from your available screensavers and set them to play back just the way you like using our concise Playback Options sheet.

 

• Newly downloaded screensavers can be added to the current playlist with a few clicks of the mouse, and "stale" screensavers deleted just as easily.

 

• Organize your screensavers by theme, and give them ratings (from 1 to 5 stars) so you can find your favorites quickly.

 

• Open the ProSaver playlist window or start your screensaver instantly with the touch of a button.

 

  And, as an added feature, have you ever thought that a certain screensaver would be just perfect ... if only it had a certain song playing in the background?  With ProSaver Screensaver Manager, you can do exactly that!  Add your favorite music to almost any screensaver, either from your own collection or from the sample audio that is included with ProSaver Screensaver Manager.  In some cases, you can even use ProSaver to change the background music already supplied with a screensaver to something that is more to your liking. The possibilities are almost endless!

 

  Numerous audio formats are supported, including MP3, WAV, MIDI, WMA, AU, and others.  You can play just one song with a particular screensaver, or you can create entire playlists of music to go perfectly with all of your favorites. You are the one running the show!

 

  As with all WaveBreak Software products, ProSaver Screensaver Manager contains NO ADWARE, NO SPYWARE, or other HIDDEN SOFTWARE, which are common annoyances found in many of the "free" screensavers and games that are available for download today. Our software is of the highest quality, and we at WaveBreak Software refuse to you with these nuisances.
