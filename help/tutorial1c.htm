Part C: The Playback Options Tab


The Playback Options Tab gives you the power to control exactly how your screensaver playlist is shown.  (Don’t let it go to your head, though.  Unless you’re the President or something.  Then you can let it go to your head.)  Here, you can do the following:

 

• Decide whether the ProSaver Playback Window should show only one screensaver each time it runs or display them as a slideshow (one after another).

 

• Determine whether you want your playlist to be shown in order, or if you would rather see random screensavers from the list.  (Hey, everyone needs a little surprise now and then.)

 

• Set keyboard shortcuts (hotkeys) to make your life just a little easier.

 

• Open the Themes Manager and/or Music Manager windows.

 

• Choose from a number of Advanced Settings.

 

 

The Playback Options Tab is described in much more detail in Tutorial #3: Setting Playback Options and Tutorial #7: Using Advanced Options.  For now, here’s something to try:

 

1. Open the ProSaver Main Window.  (Have you tried Ctrl+Alt+P yet?  Give it a try!)

 

2. Click on the Playback Options Tab at the top of the window.

 

3. In the right-hand panel (the Advanced Settings section), you will see a warning message and a button.  This is simply a notification that the options that lie within are intended for Advanced Users.

 

4. If you dare, click on the Show Advanced Options button.  (Don’t worry, if you’re not really an Advanced User, we won’t tell anyone you were looking.)

 

5. These are the Advanced Settings.  When you are done looking around, click the Hide Advanced Options button, say the magic words, and watch them disappear!  (You should try this trick at parties.  Some people will be very impressed!)
